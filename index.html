KAZAKHSTAN

Kazakhstan is a country of amazing steppe people. Since ancient times, nomadic nomads inhabited a huge country and created their own unique culture here. The originality of Kazakhstan is also determined by the fact that over the centuries local nomads have been in contact with settled neighbors in the south, Siberians in the north and Russians in the west. The amazing contrast of developed megalopolises and wild steppes makes Kazakhstan attractive even for the most sophisticated traveler.
